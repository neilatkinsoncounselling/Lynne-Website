About the space

Gentle support when you need room to think and feel.

Counselling can give you time away from the usual pressures and expectations. It can be a place to talk honestly, notice patterns, explore feelings and find a little more choice in how you respond.

The work is collaborative, respectful and paced carefully. Some people come with a clear issue. Others come because something feels heavy, confusing or stuck.

What counselling may help with

Support for the things that are difficult to carry alone.

01

Anxiety and overwhelm

Making sense of worry, pressure, panic, overthinking and the feeling that your mind will not slow down.

02

Grief and loss

A steady space for bereavement, change, endings and the quieter losses that others may not fully see.

03

Relationships and boundaries

Exploring people pleasing, conflict, family patterns, communication and the cost of always holding things together.

04

Feeling stuck

When life looks functional from the outside but inside you feel tired, disconnected or unsure what needs to change.
