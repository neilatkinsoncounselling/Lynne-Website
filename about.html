About Lynne

Warm, steady and down to earth.

Lynne offers a quieter place to talk through what is going on, without having to make it sound tidy first.

What Lynne is like to sit with

The work is gentle, but not vague. It is about slowing things down, noticing what has been hard to say and finding a way to understand it without forcing an answer.

Some people arrive knowing exactly what they want to talk about. Others arrive with a feeling, a repeated pattern or a sense that something is not right. Both are enough.
