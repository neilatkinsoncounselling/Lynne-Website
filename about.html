About me

Warm, steady and down to earth.

With me, you do not have to make things sound tidy before you bring them.

Sitting with me

I didn’t come into counselling with a plan — I came because I wanted to understand myself.

Life growing up and into adulthood wasn’t always steady, and for a long time I coped by pushing through, putting others first, and not really stopping to think about how I felt.

Over time, I started to notice patterns — in my relationships, in how much I wanted to be accepted, and how easily I could lose myself in that. Through my own therapy and training, things slowly began to make more sense.

Counselling gave me something I hadn’t always had — a space to be heard, without judgement.

That’s what led me here.

What I offer through Listening Space Counselling is simple.

A calm, supportive space where you can come exactly as you are — no pressure, no expectations, just space to talk and be heard.

Because sometimes that’s where things begin.
