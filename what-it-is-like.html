What it is like

Two chairs. Time to think.

The space I offer is deliberately simple. Nothing to perform. Nothing to get right. Just time to notice what is usually hurried, hidden or pushed aside.

When words are hard to find

You can start with fragments. A sentence. A feeling. A story that keeps circling back.

When everything feels tangled

In sessions, I can help slow the tangle down, so you can see what belongs where and what needs care first.

When you are used to carrying on

Some people are very good at coping. That does not mean it is not costing them. This can be a place where you can put some of it down.
