What it is like

Two chairs, steady attention and room to think.

The space is deliberately simple. Nothing to perform. Nothing to get right. Just time to hear what is usually hurried, hidden or pushed aside.

When words are hard to find

You can start with fragments. A sentence. A feeling. A story that keeps circling back. It does not need to be ordered before you bring it.

When everything feels tangled

Sessions can help slow the tangle down enough to see what belongs where, what matters most and what might need care first.

When you are used to carrying on

Some people are very good at coping. That does not mean it is not costing them. Counselling can be a place to stop holding everything quite so tightly.
