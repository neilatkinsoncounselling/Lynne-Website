Contact

A short message is enough.

You do not need to explain everything. A brief note about what is going on is enough to begin.

What to include

You might include what has brought you here, how you would prefer me to reply and any times that are easier for you.

I will reply as soon as I can. If your message is urgent, please use urgent support instead.

Urgent support

The Listening Space is not an emergency service. If there is immediate danger, call 999 or go to A&E. For urgent mental health support in England, contact NHS 111.
