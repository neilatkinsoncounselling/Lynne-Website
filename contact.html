Contact

Start with a few honest lines.

You do not need to explain everything in one message. A brief note about what is going on and what you are looking for is enough to begin.

What to include

You might say what has brought you here, whether you prefer a reply by email or phone and any times that are easier for Lynne to contact you.

Before publishing this page, add Lynne's confirmed contact route and privacy wording for enquiries.

Urgent support

The Listening Space is not an emergency service. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call 999 or go to A&E. For urgent mental health support in England, NHS 111 can help direct you to local support.
