First session

A first conversation, nothing more.

Our first session is a chance for you to say what has brought you here and see whether it feels like the right fit.

What you need to bring

You only need to bring yourself. Notes are fine. Silence is fine. Not knowing where to begin is fine.

What I may ask

I may ask what has been happening, what you hope might change and anything important I need to know.

What happens next

If it feels like a good fit, we can agree the next step together. If not, that is useful to know too.
