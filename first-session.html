First session

A first conversation, nothing more.

The first session is a chance to talk about what has brought you here and to get a feel for whether working together feels right.

What to bring

You only need to bring yourself. Notes are fine, silence is fine and not knowing where to begin is also fine.

What Lynne may ask

Lynne may ask what has been happening, what you want from the work and whether there is anything important she needs to know before you begin.

What happens next

If it feels like a good fit, you can agree the next step together. If it does not, that is useful information too.
